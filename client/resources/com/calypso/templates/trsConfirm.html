Maturity Payment:

The Notional Amount multiplied by the excess, if any, of the Final Price over the Initial Price (in which case the Maturity Payment will be expressed as a positive number), or by the excess, if any, of the Initial Price over the Final Price (in which case the Maturity Payment will be expressed as a negative number), as the case may be.

The Final Price is the price for the Reference Asset as calculated by the Calculation Agent in a commercially reasonable manner. In the event that Party B disputes this price, the Calculation Agent will solicit bid prices from five recognized dealers for the Reference Asset in an amount equal to the Nominal Amount. The mean bid price received without regard to the highest and lowest bid prices will be the Final Price.

For the avoidance of doubt the Initial Price includes any accrued and unpaid interest as of the Effective Date.

For the avoidance of doubt the Final Price includes any accrued and unpaid interest as of the Termination Date.
